# Demo Video Script — 90 seconds

A tight, recruiter-friendly walkthrough of **Founder School Agent**. Total runtime ≈ 90 seconds.
The voice-over is written to sound natural and confident when read aloud.

> 🎥 **A silent 90-second screen recording is already captured** at
> [`docs/demo/founder-school-agent-demo.mp4`](./docs/demo/founder-school-agent-demo.mp4) —
> it shows the live app (real GPT-4o-mini answers) moving through all three tools, timed to this
> script. Record the voice-over below over that footage, or re-shoot your own.

**Recording tips:** the captured footage runs at 1280x720. Read the voice-over at a calm pace to match
each on-screen beat, and let each result fully render before moving on. Speak warmly, not robotically.

---

## 🎬 Introduction — 10 seconds

**On screen:** The home page. Hero headline *"Your AI co-pilot from idea to event."*

**Voice-over:**

> "Founders lose weeks between having an idea and taking the next step. So I built Founder School
> Companion: an AI agent that turns intent into action. Let me show you."

---

## 🎬 Demo — 60 seconds

### Beat 1 — Founder Coach (≈ 18s)

**On screen:** Click the *Founder Coach* tab. Type: *"How do I know if my startup idea is worth
pursuing?"* and hit send. Let the reply stream in.

**Voice-over:**

> "Say I'm a founder with doubts. I ask the coach a real question, and instead of generic advice, I
> get a bold answer that ends with one concrete thing to do in the next 48 hours. Fall in love with
> the problem, talk to five real users this week. Every reply pushes me toward action."

### Beat 2 — Idea Validator (≈ 20s)

**On screen:** Switch to the *Idea Validator* tab. Paste: *"A marketplace that connects Aalto students
with local startups for paid micro-projects."* Click *Validate my idea*. Let the scored card render.

**Voice-over:**

> "Now I have an actual idea. The Validator scores it and breaks it down: the real problem, who to
> target first, the biggest risks, and three cheap experiments I can run this week. This is how you
> help a whole community move faster and fail cheaper."

### Beat 3 — Event Architect (≈ 22s)

**On screen:** Switch to the *Event Architect* tab. Type topic *"fundraising your first €100k"*, keep
format *Founder Talk*, click *Produce event plan*. Scroll slowly through the result.

**Voice-over:**

> "And this is the part built for the events role. I type one topic, and in seconds I get a complete
> event plan: a speaker brief, a full run-of-show, ready-to-post Instagram and email copy, success
> metrics, and a production checklist. Hours of work, compressed into a single click. That's real
> leverage for a small, fast-moving team."

---

## 🎬 Conclusion — 20 seconds

**On screen:** Scroll back to the top, then show the GitHub repository page.

**Voice-over:**

> "Founder School is for people of action, so instead of describing what I'd do, I shipped working
> software that does it. It runs offline with a single command, and upgrades to live AI the moment
> you add a key. The full source, documentation, and architecture are on GitHub. Idea to working
> software: that's the loop I'd bring to the Founder School team. Thanks for watching."

**On screen (end card):** *Founder School Agent · founder-school-agent.onrender.com ·
github.com/SaharPak/founder-school-agent · Sahar Pakseresht.*
