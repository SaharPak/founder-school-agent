# Elevator Pitch

A 30-second spoken pitch. Reads in about 75–85 words at a natural pace.

---

## The pitch (≈ 30 seconds)

> "Startup ecosystems run on two things: founder momentum and operator time. Both get stuck on the gap
> between an idea and the next concrete action.
>
> I built Founder School Agent to close that gap. It's an AI agent with three tools: a coach that
> pushes founders to act in the next 48 hours, a validator that turns a raw idea into honest feedback
> and cheap experiments, and an event architect that produces a full event plan in seconds.
>
> It runs offline with one command. Founder School is for people of action, so I shipped, instead of
> pitched."

---

## Even shorter (≈ 15 seconds)

> "Founder School Agent is an AI agent that helps founders take action: it coaches them, validates
> their ideas, and generates complete event plans in seconds. The job posting said Founder School is
> for people of action, so instead of describing what I'd do, I built and shipped it."

---

**Delivery notes**

- Lead with the problem, not the tool — it shows product thinking.
- Land on the closing line: it ties directly to the role and the founder mindset.
- Keep energy up and end with a confident pause, not a trailing "...yeah."
