## Version A — story-led (recommended)

> The Aalto Founder School job posting said something that stuck with me:
>
> *"Founder School is for people of action."*
>
> So instead of writing about what I would do in the role, I built it.
>
> Meet **Founder School Companion** 🚀 — an AI agent that helps founders move from idea to action to
> event. It does three things:
>
> 💬 **Founder Coach** — bold, action-first advice that always ends with one concrete next step.
> 🔍 **Idea Validator** — turns a raw idea into an honest report: the real problem, the first audience,
> the biggest risks, and cheap experiments to run this week.
> 🎤 **Event Architect** — generates a complete Founder Talk plan in seconds: speaker brief, run-of-show,
> promo copy, and success metrics.
>
> The whole thing runs offline with a single command (no API key needed) and upgrades to live AI when
> you add one. I designed it so anyone can clone it and use every feature in under five minutes.
>
> Idea → working software. That's the loop I want to bring to a fast-moving ecosystem team.
>
